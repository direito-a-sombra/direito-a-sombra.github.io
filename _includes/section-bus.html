Who Needs Shade?

The excess of light reveals climate injustices. Why haven't we built cities where shadow (or shade!) is a public infrastructure, and a social environmental right? Brazilian modern cities (and many cities around the world) were not planned for people, for animals, nor for rivers or trees.

Ms. Liduina, the woman in this photo, hides behind a thin pole shade while waiting for the bus. She commutes daily. Like many other workers. Or students. Kids, elderly.
